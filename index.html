THE PROJECT

KÖBAG a Haversack for a user with SCP (Spastic Cerebral Palsy)


Assistive technologies are a series of tools that are meant to tear down limitations in daily activities and dissolve restrictions in participation for people with disabilities.

Therefore, this collection of gadgets could make the difference in people’s lives by helping them communicate, go to school, eat, dress themselves and have leisure activities while giving them confidence and independence.

KÖBAG is a wearable device based on the universal design and intuitive paradigm, that keeps your personal belongings and school supplies safe while being trendy and practical.
